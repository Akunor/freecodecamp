Chicken Salad Recipe

This delicious Chicken Salad is easy to make, healthy and nutritious, and super tasty! It make enough for 4 people - but you can scale it up and down with no issues

Ingredients

    Salad

  • 500g Chicken (your preferred cut)
  • 2 Bay leaves
  • 200g Mixed salad leaves
  • 2 Avocados
  • 100g Cherry tomatoes
  • Sesame seed to sprinkle

    • Sesame Dressing

  • 2 tbsp Sesame Oil
  • 2 tbsp Grapeseed oil
  • 1 tbsp Honey
  • 100g Sesame seeds (toasted and ground up)
  • 1/4 cup Kewpie mayonaisse

Instructions

  1. Bring a pot of water to boil, and then place the chicken in the pot along with the bay leaves. Bring it back to a boil and then place a lid on top and turn off the heat. Leave for at least 50 minutes.
  2. Put all the dressing ingredients apart from the sesame seeds in a jar with a lid - then shake to mix.
  3. Add the ground sesame seeds to the jar, and shake further to combine. Set the jar aside.
  4. Once at least 50 minutes have passed, remove the chicken from the water and slice into 1cm thick pieces.
  5. Cut up the cherry tomatoes and avocados to your preferred size.
  6. Place the salad leaves, avocado, and tomatos into a large bowl and toss to mix.
  7. Put the chicken on top of the salad, pour over the sesame dressing, and sprinkle with sesame seeds to finish.
